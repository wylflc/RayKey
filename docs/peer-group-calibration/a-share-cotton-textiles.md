# 1. A-Share Cotton Textiles Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews cotton spinning, color-spun yarn, yarn-dyed fabric, towels, medical cotton products, and textile exporters. Ordinary cotton capacity is low-barrier; retention requires global customer qualification, product/process specialty, or a cross-industry medical-consumer platform.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain textile companies when global customer qualification, yarn/fabric process depth, or vertically integrated specialty textile capability is visible.
2. Retain cross-industry medical-consumer textile platforms when healthcare quality systems and consumer brand/channel create a distinct thesis.
3. Reject ordinary spinning, towel, fabric, and apparel-adjacent capacity when stronger retained peers cover the textile thesis.
