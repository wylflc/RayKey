# 1. A-Share Lead And Zinc Resources Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews lead-zinc mining, zinc smelting, silver-tin resource exposure, and related nonferrous resource companies.

This note records the company-by-company review for `有色金属-基本金属-铅锌`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain resource platforms with meaningful mine ownership, integrated resource position, global/strategic exposure, or scarce silver-tin/lithium/germanium optionality where capital alone cannot quickly reproduce reserves.
2. Boundary-retain smaller but real mine-right or scarce-metal companies when resource quality, by-product value, or strategic geography creates a differentiated watch thesis.
3. Reject mostly smelting, processing, engineering, or zinc-powder companies without clear reserve, cost, or monopoly-like resource advantage.
