# 1. A-Share Agricultural Machinery Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group compares tractor and agricultural-machinery platforms, outdoor power equipment, small agricultural-machine manufacturers, distributors, pumps, parts suppliers, and ST transition cases.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain agricultural-machinery platforms when brand, dealer/service network, manufacturing scale, and product breadth make displacement difficult for a funded entrant.
2. Retain boundary cross-industry equipment names only when they have a visible global channel or electrification/product platform beyond ordinary machinery.
3. Reject small farm-machine makers, distributors, pumps, parts, and ST cases when capital and channel spending could replicate the business or stronger peers already cover the thesis.
