# 1. A-Share Marine Engineering Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers marine defense electronics, underwater information systems, ocean observation, maritime intelligent navigation, and related marine-engineering equipment.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they are national marine-defense electronic platforms with scarce underwater information-system capability.
2. Retain boundary cases when ocean observation, intelligent navigation, or marine data equipment has technical validation but platform control is smaller or less certain.
3. Reject generic marine equipment when no scarce defense, qualification, or ocean-data platform is visible.
