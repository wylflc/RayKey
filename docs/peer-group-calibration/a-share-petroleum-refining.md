# 1. A-Share Petroleum Refining Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers integrated oil refining and petrochemicals, independent refining-polyester platforms, coal/oil/gas resource platforms, lubricants, asphalt, fuel-oil deep processing, and smaller petrochemical processors.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when national oil/refining/marketing infrastructure or integrated resource-to-chemical platforms are impossible to replicate with capital alone.
2. Retain boundary cases when scale, upstream resource access, port/logistics infrastructure, refining-chemical integration, or energy logistics create a materially stronger position than ordinary petroleum processing.
3. Reject lubricants, asphalt, fuel-oil processing, ST chemical cases, and subsidiary refineries when they lack scarce resources, national infrastructure, or differentiated materials beyond commodity cycle exposure.
