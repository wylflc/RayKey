# 1. A-Share Pipe-Materials Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews plastic pipe, composite pipe, and adjacent building-material companies. Most pipe manufacturing is replicable, but category brand, installer ecosystem, and terminal-channel trust can create a limited moat.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain companies only when brand, channel, installer/service ecosystem, specialty material capability, or cross-industry material exposure creates a durable advantage.
2. Reject project pipe, regional pipe, ST, and ordinary building-material manufacturers when capacity and price competition dominate.
3. Treat building-material leaders as boundary watches when long-term industry growth is weaker than technology, healthcare, and strategic-resource groups.
