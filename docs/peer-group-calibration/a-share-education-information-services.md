# 1. A-Share Education Information Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group mixes education information systems, smart campus services, vocational education, management training, textbook distribution, publishing supply chains, and ST education transition cases.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary cases when textbook/education distribution rights, content publishing, or regulated education channels create durable cash flow that a funded entrant cannot quickly reproduce.
2. Reject training, smart-campus projects, education SaaS, and mixed media/education businesses when the moat is mainly project relationships, enrollment marketing, or generic software delivery.
3. ST status and policy exposure should not automatically decide the case, but they raise the required evidence bar for durable capability.
