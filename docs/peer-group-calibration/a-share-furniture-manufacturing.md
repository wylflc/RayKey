# 1. A-Share Furniture Manufacturing Peer-Group Review
## 1.1 Purpose
This note records the company-by-company review for `轻工制造-家具-家具制造`.

This group contains whole-house customization, sofas, sleep systems, ergonomic office chairs, export furniture, flooring, wood doors, bathroom fixtures, outdoor furniture, cross-border brands, and ST furniture companies. The industry is competitive and real-estate exposed, so only brand/channel scale or global niche manufacturing leadership qualifies.
## 1.2 Retention Standard
1. Directly retain category leaders with national brand, dealer/channel density, manufacturing scale, design/system capability, and consumer trust.
2. Boundary-retain global ergonomic seating, smart sleep, or cross-border furniture platforms only when category leadership or international supply-chain/customer evidence is visible.
3. Reject ordinary furniture, flooring, doors, bathrooms, outdoor furniture, smaller custom-home brands, and ST cases where capital and marketing can reproduce the product offer.
