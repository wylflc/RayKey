# 1. A-Share Glass Fiber Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers commodity fiberglass yarn, electronic glass-fiber cloth, composite reinforcement materials, glass-fiber mats, micro-glass-fiber filtration media, and related fiberglass products.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when global scale, cost position, kiln/process know-how, overseas capacity, or multi-material platform capability is difficult for a new entrant to buy quickly.
2. Retain boundary cases when electronic-grade glass cloth, micro-glass-fiber media, customer qualification, or specialized process control creates a distinct niche not covered by commodity fiberglass leaders.
3. Reject ordinary fiberglass capacity, mats, and restructuring stories when stronger retained leaders already cover the investable thesis or when the moat is mainly cycle timing and capacity.
