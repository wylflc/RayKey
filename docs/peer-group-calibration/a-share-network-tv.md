# 1. A-Share Network TV Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews IPTV, internet TV, and provincial broadcast-network service companies. Licenses and local operator relationships can be hard for capital to copy, but growth is constrained by telecom/operator economics, content competition, and regional limits.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary companies when IPTV/internet-TV licenses, operator workflow, user data, and local monopoly-like distribution create defensible recurring economics.
2. Reject smaller or less differentiated provincial platforms when stronger retained peers already cover the license-protected IPTV thesis.
3. Do not treat broadcast licenses as high-growth moats; they justify observation only when paired with user scale and operating quality.
