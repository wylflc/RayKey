# 1. A-Share Electric Motors Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews special motors, industrial motors, motion-control motors, micro motors, wind-turbine equipment, EV drive motors, and linear-drive systems.

This note records the company-by-company review for `电气设备-电机-电机`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain motor platforms with national special-motor, large industrial-drive, or major strategic-equipment capability where qualification, reliability, process know-how, and installed customer base are hard to reproduce.
2. Boundary-retain differentiated global or specialist motor and motion-control platforms with meaningful customer validation, application depth, or cross-industry extension.
3. Reject commodity micro-motor, motor-core, consumer-appliance, linear-drive, and weaker EV-drive suppliers when stronger retained companies cover the thesis and capital can reproduce capacity.
