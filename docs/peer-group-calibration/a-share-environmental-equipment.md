# 1. A-Share Environmental Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews environmental equipment, cleanroom purification, environmental monitoring, flue-gas/water treatment, membranes, and special environmental systems.

This note records the company-by-company review for `机械设备-专用设备-环保设备`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain companies whose environmental equipment is actually a hard industrial or data-center process-control platform.
2. Boundary-retain niche technologies with semiconductors, high-grade purification, thermal cracking, heavy-metal treatment, membranes, or monitoring-system barriers.
3. Reject ordinary project contractors, generic treatment equipment, ST cases, and mature flue-gas or sanitation equipment without durable differentiation.
