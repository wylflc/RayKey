# 1. A-Share Rare Earth Remaining Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass completes remaining rare-earth companies after 北方稀土 was already retained. Rare earths have quota, separation, resource and national-security attributes; the review retains companies only when their listed-company role is not just trading or commodity beta.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain rare-earth companies with quota/resource/separation access, heavy rare-earth position, group-platform role, or overseas/resource-development differentiation.
2. Reject rare-earth names if the company lacks a distinct quota, resource, processing, or customer thesis and is only a price-cycle vehicle.
3. Multiple rare-earth companies can be retained when they represent different scarce-resource segments, such as light rare earths, medium/heavy rare earths, central-SOE platforms, or overseas/resource integration.
