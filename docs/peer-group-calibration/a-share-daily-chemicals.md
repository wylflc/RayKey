# 1. A-Share Daily-Chemicals Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group mixes consumer daily chemicals, domestic cosmetics brands, cosmetics OEMs, oral care, medical skincare, surfactants, and specialty additives. The review separates real brand or chemistry barriers from replaceable consumer-product execution.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when domestic brand-platform strength or specialty chemical process/customer barriers are visible.
2. Retain boundary consumer brands when category positioning is differentiated but competition and product cycles limit conviction.
3. Reject ordinary cosmetics OEM, weak brands, surfactants, one-product medical-dressing brands, and commodity daily-chemical manufacturers when stronger retained peers cover the thesis.

## 1.4 Sources
