# 1. A-Share Other Textiles Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group mixes nonwoven materials, automotive interior textiles, high-grade down, technical protective products, textile materials, luggage/outdoor OEM, home textiles, printing/dyeing, and ordinary fabrics. Because ordinary textile manufacturing is low-barrier, the review only retains technical material, standard-setting, safety-protection, or brand/workflow cases.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary cases when nonwoven healthcare materials, automotive interior fabrics, high-grade down standards/testing, technical protective products, or global luggage/product workflow create a niche beyond simple textile capacity.
2. Reject ordinary fabrics, printing/dyeing, home textiles, apparel retail, outdoor OEM, commodity nonwovens, and weak textile brands when the capability is easy to copy or already covered by a stronger retained peer.
3. For textiles, customer qualification and standards participation matter only when they create pricing power, switching cost, safety requirement, or durable category control.
4. A temporary cycle rebound or export order recovery is not enough for watchlist inclusion.
