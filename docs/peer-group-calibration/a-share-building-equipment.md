# 1. A-Share Building Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group mainly contains elevator companies plus a fire-safety IoT company and several ST or component cases. The review discounts generic elevator manufacturing but retains category/platform or safety-critical niches.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they are credible national elevator platforms with complete-machine, core-component, service, and brand/channel capability.
2. Retain boundary cases when elevator scale/JV position, historic local platform, elevator safety parts, or fire-safety IoT systems create a niche that is harder to replicate than generic equipment capacity.
3. Reject generic elevator manufacturers, ST cases, and mixed machinery/component companies without strong category control or safety-system differentiation.
4. Building equipment is capital- and service-intensive; watch only cases where brand, service network, safety certification, installed base, or specialized system technology matters.
