# 1. A-Share Specialty Markets Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers agricultural wholesale markets, electronics markets, home-furnishing malls, textile and leather markets, local market operators, and mixed commercial-property platforms.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary cases when a market operator controls hard-to-rebuild physical network infrastructure, merchant/customer density, or national supply-chain role.
2. Reject home-furnishing, electronics, leather, and local property markets when traffic is declining, replicable, debt-heavy, or covered by stronger market-infrastructure cases.
3. Professional markets are not automatically moats; the retained thesis must be infrastructure-like network density rather than ordinary rental property.
