# 1. A-Share Wood Processing Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers forestry assets, wood panels, container flooring, timber processing, biomass/forest-carbon stories, and ST forestry cases.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only when a company controls scarce forests, irreplaceable cost/logistics advantages, protected brands, or engineered-wood technology that competitors cannot reproduce with capital alone.
2. Reject commodity panel, timber, forestry-asset, and ST restructuring cases when earnings depend on cycles, land/resource stories, or ordinary manufacturing rather than durable business advantage.
