# 1. A-Share Scenic Spots Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews scenic-area operators and tourism-content companies. Natural scenic resources can be impossible to replicate, but growth is limited, pricing is regulated or politically sensitive, and operations are seasonal/cyclical; retention is limited to the strongest scarce-resource case.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only the clearest scarce natural scenic-resource platform when the resource is iconic and irreplaceable.
2. Reject tourism performance, local scenic operators, and weaker destinations when the advantage is brand, execution, or rebound tourism demand rather than a rare resource moat.
3. Treat scenic-area watch decisions as boundary observations because long-term compounding is weaker than technology, healthcare, and resource-platform cases.
