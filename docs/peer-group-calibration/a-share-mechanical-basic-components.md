# 1. A-Share Mechanical Basic Components Peer-Group Review
## 1.1 Purpose
This note records the company-by-company review for `机械设备-通用设备-基础件`.

This group contains hydraulic systems, nuclear and industrial valves, bearings, fasteners, reducers, precision fixtures, high-purity process components, castings, forgings, cutting tools, and many ordinary small parts. The review separates safety-critical and process-critical components from commodity machining and generic parts.
## 1.2 Retention Standard
1. Directly retain companies with global or national leadership in hydraulic systems, nuclear/special valves, high-end castings, superhard bearings/tools, high-purity process components, or other safety-critical parts.
2. Boundary-retain narrower leaders in rail fasteners, joint bearings, precision fixtures, automation components, industrial valves, high-end tools, large forgings, aerospace vessels, or wind-drive parts when customer qualification is visible.
3. Reject ordinary bearings, gears, cylinders, shafts, home-appliance parts, molds, generic reducers, and ST cases without scarce qualification or irreplaceable process know-how.
