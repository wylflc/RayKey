## 1.2 Retention Standard

2. Reject strong regional banks when the moat is mainly local economy beta, current ROE, or asset-quality cycle rather than a distinct operating model.
3. Treat these rejected banks as possible valuation/dividend studies later, but not as first-round quality-watch additions.
