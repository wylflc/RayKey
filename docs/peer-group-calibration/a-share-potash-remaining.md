# 1. A-Share Remaining Potash Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group completes the remaining potash names after 盐湖股份 and 藏格矿业 had already been retained under the resource standard.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain potash companies when controlled reserves, cost curve, production expansion, country/resource access, or scarce domestic/import-substitution role creates a resource moat that capital alone cannot quickly reproduce.
2. Reject mixed industrial companies when potash exposure is indirect, smaller, or already covered by cleaner retained potash and salt-lake resource platforms.
3. Compare resource durability rather than one-year profit, but require evidence of resource control and operating path.
