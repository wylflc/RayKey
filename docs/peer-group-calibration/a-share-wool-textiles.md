# 1. A-Share Wool Textiles Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews wool textiles, apparel-adjacent textile processing, and one cross-industry new-material case. Ordinary wool textiles are low-growth and manufacturing-capacity driven; retention requires a material hard-material or technical-fiber angle, not only garment fabric execution.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain textile companies only when cross-industry new-material capability or a hard-to-copy technical fiber niche is material.
2. Reject ordinary wool yarn, garment fabric, apparel, or cultural/film mixed exposure when capital and customer execution can reproduce the business.
3. Apply cross-industry advantage review before rejecting companies whose official business includes non-textile high-performance materials.
