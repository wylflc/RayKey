# 1. A-Share Cloud, IDC, And CDN Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `互联网-互联网技术-云服务(含IDC、CDN)`.

The group includes enterprise-cloud software, CDN and edge-network infrastructure, data-center operators, cloud-network providers, game-to-cloud transition cases, and ST cloud/AI stories. The review separates workflow/software moats and operating infrastructure scarcity from businesses that are mainly capital-intensive server capacity or cloud-label narratives.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain companies that own hard-to-replicate software workflow or network infrastructure: enterprise management software with high switching costs, or CDN/edge/security networks with global nodes, traffic scheduling, customer integration, and operational history.
2. Retain boundary IDC and cloud infrastructure companies only when they show more than capital spending: core-city resources, hyperscale or enterprise customer relationships, data-center energy efficiency, reliable delivery record, cloud partnerships, global cloud-network nodes, or integrated product/operation capability.
3. Reject companies whose cloud exposure is mainly a label attached to games, content, system integration, or small cloud/AI products without verified scale, customer lock-in, clean governance, or infrastructure scarcity.
4. Do not reject a company only because the current cloud transition is unprofitable. Do reject when a well-funded new entrant or larger cloud platform could reproduce the business without needing the company's software workflow, network, location, power, customer, or regulatory asset.
