# 1. A-Share Insurance Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group compares A-share listed insurance groups. Insurance has license, brand, capital, distribution, actuarial, asset-liability management, and customer-trust barriers, but also interest-rate, reserve, investment-cycle, and agency-channel risks.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies with nationwide brand, customer base, capital strength, integrated financial services, asset-management capability, and differentiated channel or ecosystem advantages.
2. Retain boundary cases when a company has a clear segment leadership in life insurance, property and casualty insurance, health/retirement services, or asset management but compounding quality is less certain.
3. Reject weaker life-insurance peers when scale, channel quality, investment capability, or ecosystem differentiation is clearly below retained peers.
4. Insurance watchlist inclusion requires durable franchise quality, not merely an insurance license or near-term investment spread recovery.
