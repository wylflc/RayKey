# 1. A-Share Road And Bridge Construction Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers road, bridge, tunnel, port, municipal and highway construction companies. Most construction businesses are low-margin and working-capital heavy, but a few global engineering platforms and tunnel specialists have hard project experience and technical systems.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when global infrastructure, ports, dredging, bridge/tunnel engineering, design and project management create capability that local contractors cannot quickly copy.
2. Retain boundary cases when tunnel or underground infrastructure specialization is credible but construction margins and project cycles limit compounding quality.
3. Reject local road/bridge contractors, ST companies, and generic infrastructure builders when their edge is mainly bidding relationships and working capital.

## 1.4 Sources

This first-pass peer-group review uses the local full-coverage score fields as background metadata and records official exchange disclosure landing pages in each decision row. Aggregator company introductions are not treated as decisive evidence.
