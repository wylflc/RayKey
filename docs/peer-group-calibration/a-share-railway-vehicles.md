# 1. A-Share Railway Vehicles Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group contains the listed railway vehicle platform. Rail vehicles, high-speed trains, locomotives and metro rolling stock require system integration, safety certification, installed base, maintenance workflow and state-level project history.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain railway vehicle platforms when safety certification, system integration, installed base, maintenance network and global project history are central.
2. Do not reject rail equipment leaders only because procurement is cyclical or state-owned; the barrier is certification and system integration.
3. Use this group as the rolling-stock counterpart to previously retained rail signaling and underground equipment companies.
