## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain high-precision BeiDou/GNSS platforms with core algorithms, chips/modules, correction networks, and broad industrial applications.
2. Boundary-retain older satellite-navigation or aerospace-chip platforms when the capability is strategic but execution, governance, or customer concentration lowers conviction.
3. Reject ST or unresolved risk-warning cases unless their strategic capability is strong enough to overcome governance and operating risk in a later dedicated review.
