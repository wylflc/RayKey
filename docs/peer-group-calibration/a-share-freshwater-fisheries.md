# 1. A-Share Freshwater Fisheries Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews freshwater aquaculture and seafood processing/trading. The default is low-barrier rejection because breeding, processing, cold-chain, and retail execution are generally replicable unless genetics, scarce water rights, or brand/channel economics are unusually strong.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Reject aquaculture and seafood processing when the advantage is commodity supply, processing capacity, or channel execution.
2. Retain only when scarce water resources, genetics/seed technology, disease-control know-how, or a dominant brand/channel system is clearly proven.
3. Do not retain a company merely because protein demand is stable.
