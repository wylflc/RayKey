# 1. A-Share Recreational Goods Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers pianos, outdoor and camping products, pet products, amusement equipment, fitness equipment, sports facilities, toys, and other recreational goods.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only if a company has a durable global brand, proprietary IP, specialized technology, or customer ecosystem that a funded entrant cannot reproduce through contract manufacturing and marketing.
2. Reject ordinary consumer recreational goods, OEM/ODM products, toys, pet products, pianos, and fitness equipment when demand is discretionary, product cycles are short, and brand/technology moats are weak.
