# 1. A-Share Internal Combustion Engine Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers diesel and internal-combustion engine manufacturers, small-engine suppliers, and related power-equipment companies.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only if an engine company controls a scarce platform with strong after-sales ecosystem, emissions technology, or a differentiated transition path into hybrid, gas, or new-energy power systems.
2. Reject ordinary internal-combustion engine manufacturers when demand is structurally pressured and technology, channel, and cost advantages are not strong enough versus larger powertrain peers.
