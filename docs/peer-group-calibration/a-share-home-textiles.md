# 1. A-Share Home Textiles Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews home textile brands, bedding, silk products, blankets, and weak home-furnishing names. Home textiles have brand and channel value but weak technology and limited growth, so retention is limited to clearly recognized brands with product/design and channel differentiation.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only home-textile brands with clear national brand recognition, channel depth, and differentiated design or category positioning.
2. Reject smaller or weaker brands when the thesis is mainly retail recovery, e-commerce execution, or ordinary textile manufacturing.
3. Keep retained home-textile names as boundary consumer-brand observations because long-term category growth and technical barriers are modest.
