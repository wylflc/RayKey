## 1.1 Purpose

This pass completes the still-undecided tungsten companies after 厦门钨业 and 中钨高新 were already retained in the strategic-resource calibration. The review asks whether each company adds a differentiated tungsten-resource or hard-alloy thesis beyond those stronger retained platforms.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain a tungsten company only when reserve access, quota-constrained upstream supply, downstream carbide/tooling know-how, customer qualification, or integrated chain capability is visible.
2. Reject tungsten processors when the thesis is mainly APT, powder, smelting, or commodity processing that can be competed away by stronger retained tungsten platforms.
3. Do not use temporary tungsten price strength as a watchlist reason unless company-level scarcity or processing capability is also evident.
