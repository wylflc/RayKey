# 1. A-Share Coatings And Pigments Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews architectural coatings, industrial coatings, pigments, pearlescent materials, and coating resins. Ordinary coating capacity is easy to copy; retention requires strong consumer/engineering brand channels or specialty pigment/material process advantages.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only coating companies with clear national brand/channel systems or specialty pigment/material process capability.
2. Reject ordinary coating, ink, resin, and project coating producers when capacity and customer relationships are replicable.
3. Separate brand-led architectural coatings from technology-led specialty pigment/material niches; both remain boundary unless dominance is exceptional.
