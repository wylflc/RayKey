# 1. A-Share Other Railway Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group contains rail vehicle interiors/doors/accessories and rail signaling/industrial rail control systems. The review separates passenger-vehicle component qualification from safety-critical signaling and control systems.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary companies when railway vehicle interiors, platform doors, passenger systems, or rail-signal/dispatch products require qualification and installed project history.
2. Give higher weight to safety-critical signaling and dispatch systems than to ordinary interior components.
3. Reject if railway equipment exposure is ordinary manufacturing without certification, installed-base, or customer switching cost.
