# 1. A-Share Jewelry Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group contains heritage jewelry brands, gold retailers, fashion jewelry, diamond brands, watches, and weak turnaround retailers. Jewelry has brand and channel elements, but most listed companies are exposed to gold-price traffic, franchising, and consumer-cycle competition rather than hard moats.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only category-defining heritage or brand/channel leaders whose consumer trust and store network would be hard to rebuild quickly.
2. Reject gold retail, franchise jewelry, diamond/fashion brands, watches, and turnaround cases when the moat is mainly store count, marketing, or gold-price demand.
3. Do not retain multiple similar jewelry retailers unless a second company has a clearly different and durable brand or product moat.
