# 1. A-Share Public Bus And Local Passenger Transport Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews road passenger transport, taxi/cold-chain/logistics, local bus and metro operating assets, and regional transport companies.

This note records the company-by-company review for `交通运输-公路铁路-公交`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Direct-watch is generally inappropriate for this group because bus and road passenger transport are regulated, low-growth, labor-intensive, and exposed to structural traffic loss.
2. Boundary-retain only genuinely scarce urban rail or franchise-like transport assets where route/franchise control is hard to reproduce.
3. Reject road passenger, taxi, logistics, and mixed tourism-transport operators when local permits and fleets do not create a strong compounding moat.
