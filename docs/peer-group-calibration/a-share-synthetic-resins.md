# 1. A-Share Synthetic Resins Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers specialty epoxy resins, vinyl chloride copolymer resins, nylon and industrial-fiber resin chains, ion-exchange and adsorption resins, electronic resins, powder-coating polyester resins, composite/wind resins, electrical insulation resins, PEEK, and ordinary unsaturated polyester resin.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary cases when resin chemistry, customer qualification, electronic/electrical insulation use, ion-exchange/adsorption function, wind/composite certification, or high-performance polymer routes create durable barriers.
2. Retain direct-watch only for genuinely scarce high-performance polymer platforms where process know-how and application qualification are very hard to buy quickly.
3. Reject nylon commodity capacity, powder-coating resin, ordinary unsaturated polyester resin, and mixed transition stories when stronger specialty-material peers cover the durable thesis.
