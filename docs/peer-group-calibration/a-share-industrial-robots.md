# 1. A-Share Industrial Robots Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews industrial robots, motion-control systems, embodied-intelligence equipment, power robots, special robots, and intelligent manufacturing integrators.

This note records the company-by-company review for `机械设备-机器人-工业机器人`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain industrial automation or robotics platforms with core controls, servos, robot bodies, broad installed base, and domestic-substitution relevance; current profit weakness alone should not exclude them.
2. Boundary-retain special-application robot companies when they hold a mission-critical power, nuclear, defense, welding, or industrial niche not covered by broader leaders.
3. Reject generic system integrators, small automation-line suppliers, and weaker robot followers when stronger retained companies cover the same thesis.
