# 1. A-Share Textile And Apparel Machinery Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group compares sewing, cutting, spinning, knitting, dyeing, and other textile machinery suppliers. The review keeps global or specialized intelligent-equipment platforms and rejects generic textile machinery capacity.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they have global market leadership, intelligent sewing/software-hardware integration, service networks, and full apparel-equipment platform capability.
2. Retain boundary cases when they own global heritage brands, spinning machinery technology, computerized knitting control, or a narrow textile-machinery niche with standards and customer lock-in.
3. Reject ST cases, generic dyeing/finishing machinery, ordinary textile equipment, and weaker sewing-machine manufacturers when stronger retained platforms cover the thesis.
4. Textile machinery inclusion requires process know-how, software/control capability, global channel, installed-base service, or standards leadership.
