# 1. A-Share Auto Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews auto testing, proving grounds, auto engineering design, taxi/driving-school services, and related service names. Most auto services are project or local-service businesses; retention requires accredited testing, scarce proving-ground assets, or safety/quality validation barriers.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain auto-service companies when accredited testing, certification, proving-ground assets, or safety-critical validation creates a barrier.
2. Reject ordinary auto design outsourcing, taxi/local mobility, driving schools, and generic auto-service providers when capital and hiring can reproduce the business.
3. Keep retained names as boundary cases unless the platform is clearly national and irreplaceable.
