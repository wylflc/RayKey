# 1. A-Share Airports Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews listed airport operators and airport-related mixed infrastructure platforms.

This note records the company-by-company review for `交通运输-航空机场-机场`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain major hub airports where location, route network, passenger/cargo flow, and commercial concession economics are hard to reproduce with capital alone.
2. Boundary-retain regional or secondary hub airports when the asset is scarce but tariff regulation, duty-free/commercial cycle, or growth constraints cap conviction.
3. Reject mixed airport/property platforms when non-airport assets, historical balance-sheet issues, or unclear economics dilute the airport moat.
