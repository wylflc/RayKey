# 1. A-Share Other Nonbank Finance Peer-Group Review
## 1.1 Purpose
This note records the company-by-company review for `金融-非银行金融-其他非银行金融`.

This group contains SOE financial holding platforms, aircraft leasing, financial leasing, futures brokers, distressed-asset managers, local financial holding companies, investment platforms, department-store/payment cases, and pawn/microfinance businesses. The review retains only scarce licenses, group-captive platforms, global leasing scale, or distinctive distressed/futures franchises.
## 1.2 Retention Standard
1. Boundary-retain finance platforms when they control scarce licenses, large captive industrial customers, national or global leasing scale, or differentiated distressed-asset/futures ecosystems.
2. Reject generic local financial holding companies, mixed industrial/investment shells, small futures companies, pawn/microfinance, department-store/payment stories, and asset-light investment narratives without durable franchise quality.
3. Because most nonbank-finance earnings are cycle and leverage sensitive, this group has boundary rather than direct-watch decisions unless the franchise is exceptionally irreplaceable.
