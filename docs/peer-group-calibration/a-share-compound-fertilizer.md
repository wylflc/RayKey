# 1. A-Share Compound Fertilizer Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group compares compound-fertilizer brands, phosphate-resource integrated producers, agricultural-service distributors, controlled-release fertilizer niches, and mixed fertilizer/chemical companies.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary cases when fertilizer economics are backed by phosphate/salt resources, integrated phosphorus chemistry, or differentiated fertilizer technology with enough scale.
2. Reject pure formulation, distribution, brand advertising, and weak regional fertilizer producers where a funded entrant can replicate capacity and channels.
3. Do not retain fertilizer companies solely for current crop-cycle profits; require resource position, process integration, or hard-to-copy agronomic technology.
