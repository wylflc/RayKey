# 1. A-Share Other-Fibers Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group is labelled as other fibers but mixes commodity synthetic fibers with carbon fiber, UHMWPE, glass-fiber composites, and upstream high-performance fiber materials. It therefore requires cross-industry review rather than a blanket chemical-cycle rejection.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain high-performance carbon fiber, UHMWPE, precursor, and qualified composite-material companies when process stability, product grade, customer qualification, or defense/aerospace/industrial validation creates hard barriers.
2. Reject commodity polyester, polypropylene, nylon, recycled fiber, and functional textile companies when scale or product wording is the main advantage.
3. Retain multiple companies only when their roles are differentiated: carbon-fiber platform, high-end niche, scaled producer, precursor supplier, UHMWPE protective-material niche, or glass-fiber composite qualification.
4. Do not reject a high-performance material company solely because current profitability or industry supply is cyclical.
