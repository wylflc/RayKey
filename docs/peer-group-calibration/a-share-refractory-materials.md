# 1. A-Share Refractory Materials Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews refractory materials, magnesia resources, industrial high-temperature insulation, and steel/cement refractory-service companies. The group is cyclical and often low-margin, so retention requires scarce mineral input, high-temperature material know-how, standards/research depth, or a differentiated insulation platform.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain resource-backed refractory companies when magnesite or similar input scarcity materially supports the moat.
2. Retain specialty high-temperature insulation or research-platform cases when material know-how is harder to copy than ordinary refractory production.
3. Reject steel-cycle refractory service companies when the advantage is mainly customer service, capacity, or current margins rather than durable product/process leadership.
