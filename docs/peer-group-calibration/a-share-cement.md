# 1. A-Share Cement Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews cement, aggregate, concrete, and related building-material platforms. Cement has severe property-cycle and overcapacity headwinds, so retention is limited to exceptional cost, regional discipline, aggregate/international optionality, or dominant platform quality.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only exceptional cement platforms with durable cost/scale, regional discipline, aggregate/new-material extension, or international expansion.
2. Reject ordinary regional cement and concrete companies even when current cash flow or asset value looks acceptable.
3. Classify retained cement names as mature cyclical/resource-platform observations rather than compounding growth companies.
