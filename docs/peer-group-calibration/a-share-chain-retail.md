# 1. A-Share Chain-Retail Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews specialty chain retail, snack/consumer chain, phone distribution, home-improvement retail, liquor retail, and mother-and-baby retail. Store expansion alone is not a moat.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only category-defining chain platforms with hard brand, supply-chain, membership, or proprietary-product control.
2. Reject chain retailers when the model can be copied through capital, leases, sourcing, and operating teams.
3. Reject fast-expansion formats when differentiation has not survived a full cycle.
