# 1. A-Share Third-Party Payment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews independent third-party payment and merchant acquiring companies. Payment licenses, merchant acceptance networks, risk systems, and small-merchant data can create barriers, but dominant super-app payment ecosystems cap the moat of independent players.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain independent payment companies only when license, merchant network, risk system, and small-business service ecosystem are visible.
2. Reject distressed or weak payment/fintech companies when regulatory risk and governance overwhelm the license value.
3. Keep retained payment names as boundary watches unless the company controls an irreplaceable payment network.
