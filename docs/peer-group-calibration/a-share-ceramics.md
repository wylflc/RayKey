# 1. A-Share Ceramics Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews ceramic tiles, sanitary ceramics, daily ceramics, and kaolin resource companies. Ceramic tiles and sanitaryware are property-cycle and brand-channel businesses with limited moat; resource-backed kaolin can be a different thesis.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only the strongest ceramic-tile brand/channel platform when category leadership is visible despite property-cycle headwinds.
2. Retain kaolin/resource-backed companies when the mineral resource is material and differentiated from ordinary ceramic product manufacturing.
3. Reject weaker tile, sanitaryware, daily-ceramic, and distressed ceramic producers when capital and channels can replicate the business.
