# 1. A-Share Audio Visual Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group compares display brands, TV/LED/ODM platforms, laser-display technology, and an ST digital-TV equipment case. The review separates brand and core optical/display technology from ordinary assembly.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies with global display brand strength, core display technology, original laser/light-source patents, or differentiated automotive/cinema/projector applications.
2. Retain boundary cases when TV/LED/ODM scale, components, and consumer brand expansion are meaningful but less differentiated than direct-watch technology or brand leaders.
3. Reject ST or legacy digital-TV equipment cases where operating risk and weak market position dominate.
4. Audio-visual equipment needs brand, core optics/display technology, channel, or component platform control rather than assembly capacity alone.
