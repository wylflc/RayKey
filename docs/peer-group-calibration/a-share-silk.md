# 1. A-Share Silk Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers silk manufacturing, silk trade/export, cultural silk products, and customized silk consumption.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only if brand, channel, design IP, or process technology creates pricing power and repeat demand that cannot be quickly copied by a funded textile entrant.
2. Reject silk export, trade, and cultural-consumption niches when the moat is mostly heritage, product variety, or discretionary demand without durable pricing power.
