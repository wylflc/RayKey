# 1. A-Share Other Liquor Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews prepared cocktails and non-baijiu liquor names. It follows the prior alcohol rule: mature or fragile alcohol categories should retain only the strongest category representative, and brand durability must be weighed against changing consumer preference.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only the clear category leader when the product is brand-led but technically replicable.
2. Reject weak, distressed, or unfocused alcohol brands even if the category has occasional rebound potential.
3. Keep the decision separate from valuation or short-term channel inventory cycles.
