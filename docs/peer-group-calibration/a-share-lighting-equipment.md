# 1. A-Share Lighting Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews professional lighting, commercial lighting, home lighting, stage lighting, and lighting components.

This note records the company-by-company review for `家电-照明设备-照明设备`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain professional or safety-critical lighting platforms where product reliability, channel/service, or application expertise is hard to reproduce.
2. Boundary-retain category leaders or specialist global niches when brand, channel, certification, or application know-how is distinct.
3. Reject ordinary LED, ODM/OEM, consumer lighting, and component businesses when competition is low-barrier and easily replicated by funded entrants.
