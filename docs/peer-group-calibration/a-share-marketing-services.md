# 1. A-Share Marketing Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews digital marketing agencies, cross-border advertising, e-commerce operation services, brand management, content marketing, and related advertising-service companies.

This note records the company-by-company review for `文化传媒-营销服务-营销服务`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Direct-watch is intentionally rare because ordinary advertising agency, media buying, and e-commerce operation work is low-barrier and can be replicated with capital, hiring, and media relationships.
2. Boundary-retain only marketing-technology or global advertising platforms with differentiated overseas media access, data/algorithm tooling, scaled client base, or AI-enabled workflow capability beyond project execution.
3. Reject broad marketing agencies, livestream/e-commerce operators, PR/event providers, legacy ad companies, ST cases, and content/IP stories when the moat is mainly relationships, execution labor, or media resale rather than hard platform control.
