# 1. A-Share Integrated Power Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group compares broad power-equipment platforms with power-electronics and power-service companies. The key question is whether the listed company controls hard equipment, grid/generation technology, or system-level product platforms rather than ordinary EPC or operating services.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain companies with power-electronics platforms, generation-equipment breadth, grid/generation-system qualification, or global industrial equipment capability.
2. Reject power-service and EPC/operator models when the business is more project execution than scarce product capability.
3. Broad state or industrial platforms are watched only when the listed company itself carries equipment technology and customer qualification, not merely because the group is large.
4. A new entrant with capital should still face product certification, operating track record, installed-base trust, and grid/generation customer validation barriers.
