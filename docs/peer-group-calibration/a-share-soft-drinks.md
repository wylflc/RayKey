# 1. A-Share Soft Drinks Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews energy drinks, plant-protein drinks, bottled water, fruit-juice concentrate, and packaged beverage brands.

This note records the company-by-company review for `食品饮料-饮料-软饮料`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain beverage companies with national brand, channel density, repeated-purchase category leadership, and multi-product extension evidence.
2. Boundary-retain category-defining plant-protein or scarce-water-resource brands when the niche is distinct from stronger retained peers.
3. Reject regional, commodity, fad, or concentrate processors when branding/channel moat is insufficient or a stronger retained peer covers the thesis.
