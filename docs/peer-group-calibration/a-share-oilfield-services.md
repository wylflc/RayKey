# 1. A-Share Oilfield Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers offshore oil engineering, drilling and well services, oilfield equipment, petroleum engineering EPC, module construction, exploration technology, and ST or small regional oil-service cases.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when offshore engineering platforms, integrated offshore service fleets, or core equipment capability are difficult to reproduce with capital alone.
2. Retain boundary cases when oilfield equipment, engineering delivery, or integrated drilling plus resource exposure creates a differentiated position, but the company is still cycle and capex exposed.
3. Reject small regional oilfield services, generic EPC, module fabrication, ST cases, and weaker duplicate SOE service arms when stronger retained platforms cover the durable thesis.
