# 1. A-Share Iron-Ore Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews A-share companies classified under iron ore. The group is mostly commodity-resource exposure, so the review applies the strategic-resource rule: do not retain a company merely because it owns mines or benefits from commodity prices.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only companies with scarce reserve quality, durable low-cost position, development capability, processing advantage, or a cross-industry company-level thesis that the narrow iron-ore label misses.
2. Reject ordinary domestic iron-ore beta when global majors dominate cost and scale.
3. Do not use current profit weakness as the primary rejection reason; the key question is whether capital alone can quickly replicate or overpower the company.
