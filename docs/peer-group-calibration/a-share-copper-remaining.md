# 1. A-Share Copper Remaining Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass completes remaining copper-group companies after prior strategic-resource calibration already decided 紫金矿业, 江西铜业 and 云南铜业. The review separates scarce-resource platforms and high-performance copper materials from smelting scale and commodity copper processing.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain copper companies only when there is scarce reserve ownership, mine-development capability, low-cost resource exposure, or special copper-alloy/material technology.
2. Reject copper smelting, trading, tube/strip/bar processing, and ordinary commodity exposure when revenue scale does not prove a moat.
3. Consider cross-industry resource packages before rejection; a company with multi-metal resource economics may deserve retention even if the narrow copper label understates it.
