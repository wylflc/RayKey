## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews domestic wine companies. Chinese wine has brand heritage in a few names, but category growth, younger-consumer penetration and import/substitution competition are weak; the watchlist should keep only the category representative if any.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only a category leader with long brand history, channel recognition, vineyard/winery assets, and consumer memory.
2. Reject ST, weak regional, or follower wine companies whose brand and operating quality do not survive comparison with the retained representative.
3. Do not retain wine companies for possible cycle rebound alone.
