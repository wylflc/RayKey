# 1. A-Share Hotels Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews hotel-chain and hotel-property operators. Hotels are service and real-estate-cycle exposed; capital can add rooms, so retention requires a national brand/franchise system, membership scale, and operating platform that smaller chains cannot reproduce quickly.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only the strongest national hotel-chain platform when brands, franchise systems, and membership/channel scale are visible.
2. Reject small hotel operators and local hotel-property companies when their advantage is location, property ownership, or cyclical travel recovery.
3. Treat hotel watch decisions as boundary consumer-service observations, not high-moat technology or resource cases.
