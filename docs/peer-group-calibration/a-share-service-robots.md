# 1. A-Share Service Robots Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews consumer service robot platforms. The category has hardware competition and consumer-cycle volatility, but leading companies can build moats through navigation algorithms, product iteration, supply chain, brand, app/ecosystem, and overseas channel.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain service-robot leaders when navigation/AI, product iteration, supply chain, brand, and global channels are visible.
2. Allow multiple retained leaders when their channels, product architecture, and brand positioning are meaningfully different.
3. Reject future service-robot names that lack product iteration or brand/channel validation even if the industry is growing.
