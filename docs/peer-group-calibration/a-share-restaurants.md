# 1. A-Share Restaurants Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews listed restaurant operators. Restaurant businesses can have local brands and recovery cycles, but many earn execution returns rather than durable compounding returns.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only restaurant companies with category-defining national brands, supply-chain control, franchise economics, or platform power that capital cannot quickly replicate.
2. Reject regional restaurant brands, legacy brands with weak modern execution, ST cases, and store models that can be copied through capital and operating teams.
3. Allow a whole-group rejection when the industry lacks durable company-level barriers.
