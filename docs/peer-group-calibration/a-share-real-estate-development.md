# 1. A-Share Real Estate Development Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group is mostly property developers, commercial property owners, industrial park operators, and distressed real-estate companies. The review treats ordinary real-estate development as structurally low quality for this watchlist, but still checks for misclassified companies whose real business has moved into technology or strategic materials.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Reject ordinary residential and commercial developers when their advantage is land bank, leverage, local relationships, or cycle timing rather than a durable operating moat.
2. Reject industrial-park and commercial-property owners unless the listed company has a separately observable technology, platform, data, or operating capability that can compound beyond property assets.
3. Retain misclassified companies only when the current business has a clear non-real-estate capability such as semiconductor equipment, strategic materials, or other hard-to-replicate technology.

## 1.4 Sources
