# 1. A-Share Department-Stores Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews department stores, shopping centers, and mixed retail operators. Most companies earn local retail and property-operation returns; retention requires a scarce license, national platform, or hard-to-replicate channel control.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain regulated duty-free platforms when license scarcity, procurement scale, and travel-retail channels are the core moat.
2. Reject ordinary department stores and shopping centers when location, leasing, and merchandising can be replicated by capital and local execution.
3. Reject weaker duty-free license holders when the evidence does not show scale or channel strength comparable with the retained national platform.
