This group contains national dairy leaders, regional low-temperature milk companies, cheese brands, infant-formula companies, baking ingredients, and small local dairy firms. ## 1.2 Retention Standard
1. Directly retain national dairy leaders with brand, channel, supply-chain, product breadth, and quality-control advantages.
2. Boundary-retain companies with differentiated fresh-milk, cheese, or high-end regional/category positions that are not simply local milk capacity.
3. Reject small regional dairies, weak infant-formula brands, generic dairy processors, and adjacent food suppliers without durable brand/channel leadership.
