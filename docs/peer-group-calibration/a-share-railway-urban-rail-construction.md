# 1. A-Share Railway And Urban Rail Construction Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews railway construction contractors, urban-rail design, and electrified-rail equipment companies.

This note records the company-by-company review for `建筑-基础建设-铁路城轨建设`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain global railway/infrastructure contractors where qualification, execution scale, resource integration, and overseas capability are hard to reproduce.
2. Boundary-retain specialized urban-rail design or electrification equipment companies with scarce qualification and line-use history.
3. Reject smaller contractors when the business is project/capex-driven and the thesis is covered by stronger national platforms.
