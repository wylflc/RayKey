# 1. A-Share Other Professional Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `休闲、生活及专业服务-专业服务-其他专业服务`.

This is a mixed residual professional-services group. It includes comprehensive TIC platforms, electrical and automotive national testing institutes, irradiation sterilization, semiconductor production services, construction-science institutes, engineering design houses, HR outsourcing, equipment rental, smart-city integrators, municipal services, exhibition and creative-design companies, tender/procurement agencies, and other project-based consulting businesses.

The review does not treat current profit as the primary filter. The central test is whether a well-funded new entrant could quickly reproduce the company's authorization, lab assets, process know-how, customer validation, standards role, regulated access, operating network, or workflow control.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain platforms with hard-to-copy regulated or technical infrastructure: national electrical and high-voltage testing institutes, leading full-chain TIC platforms, automotive engineering and testing platforms, irradiation sterilization networks, semiconductor failure-analysis platforms, and proprietary engineering process-package companies.
2. Retain boundary companies when a niche is independently differentiated from retained leaders: construction-science institutes with special laboratories, semiconductor equipment cleaning, nonferrous metallurgy engineering, grid or cable specialty testing, aerospace reliability testing, strategic-mineral testing, food-security engineering, equipment-rental scale operations, or a uniquely scaled compliance-outsourcing platform.
3. Reject project-based, labor-intensive, or relationship-driven businesses where the disclosed advantage is mainly资质, awards, local references, hired talent, fleet capital, or tender execution rather than a scarce technical or regulated platform.
4. For repeated sub-sectors, retain only companies with distinct capabilities. For example, comprehensive TIC leaders can coexist with national high-voltage labs, semiconductor analysis labs, and cable testing centers, but ordinary regional inspection firms are rejected when stronger retained peers already cover the thesis.
