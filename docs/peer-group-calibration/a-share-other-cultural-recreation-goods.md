# 1. A-Share Other Cultural-Recreation Goods Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews discretionary cultural, toy, and fitness-equipment goods. These products usually face design, channel, and price competition, so the default standard is strict.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only companies with strong IP, category-defining brand power, proprietary product ecosystems, or manufacturing capabilities that capital cannot quickly copy.
2. Reject discretionary product manufacturers when brand power is weak and products can be replicated by stronger brands or lower-cost manufacturers.
3. Allow whole-group rejection when neither company-level IP nor hard manufacturing moat is visible.
