# 1. A-Share Auto Sales Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group contains automobile distribution, import/export, dealership, trading, and mixed local-service companies. The industry is normally asset- and working-capital-heavy, manufacturer-dependent, and low in durable company-level barriers.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Reject ordinary auto sales, dealership, trading, import/export and mixed local-service businesses unless there is a clear proprietary platform, data network, exclusive license, or irreplaceable service workflow.
2. Scale alone is not enough because manufacturer policy, inventory cycle and price competition dominate economics.
3. A company should not enter the watchlist merely because new-energy vehicles create industry growth if its own role is distribution rather than technology or manufacturing capability.
