# 1. A-Share Steel Structure Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers steel-structure manufacturing, prefabricated building systems, large-span spatial structures, industrial buildings, and ST or smaller regional steel-structure contractors.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary cases when manufacturing scale, automated plants, design/manufacturing standards, project references, and delivery network create a cost/service position that is not easily copied by capital alone.
2. Reject smaller steel-structure contractors and ST cases when they lack scale, standardized manufacturing, or differentiated project references beyond bidding and fabrication capacity.
3. Steel structure is still construction-cycle exposed, so even leaders are boundary unless they show exceptional global platform economics.
