# 1. A-Share Construction Machinery Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews construction machinery, industrial vehicles, micro-drive systems, engineering seals, and related equipment companies.

This note records the company-by-company review for `机械设备-专用设备-工程机械`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain global construction-machinery leaders with scale, product breadth, overseas channel, manufacturing process, and service-network advantages.
2. Boundary-retain differentiated component, industrial vehicle, logistics automation, or niche equipment companies with customer qualification or category leadership.
3. Reject smaller cyclical equipment makers when their thesis is already covered by stronger retained leaders or when the business is mostly capacity/project exposure.
