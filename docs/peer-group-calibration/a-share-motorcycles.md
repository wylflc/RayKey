# 1. A-Share Motorcycles Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews motorcycle manufacturers and recreational/off-road motorcycle names. The industry has brand and engineering elements, but many products remain capital-replicable; retention requires proven high-displacement brand, product iteration, distribution, and export capability.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain motorcycle companies when brand, engine/product iteration, dealer network, and export or premium-category validation are visible.
2. Reject small recreational or generic motorcycle/engine companies when they lack an irreplaceable niche versus retained leaders.
3. Treat current sales scale as insufficient unless paired with durable product and brand capability.
