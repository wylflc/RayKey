## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain companies with global animal-nutrition, amino-acid, or feed platform leadership where scale, R&D, cost curve, and channel are hard to reproduce.
2. Boundary-retain differentiated pet-food, biological additive, aquatic-feed, or seed/ag-biotech cross-industry cases when the capability is not fully covered by retained leaders.
3. Reject commodity feed, pig-cycle, regional aquafeed, and integrated farming companies when a stronger retained peer covers the thesis and moat is weak.
