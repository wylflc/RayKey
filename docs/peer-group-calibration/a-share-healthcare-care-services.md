# 1. A-Share Healthcare Care Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group contains a mixed healthcare-care-service/conglomerate case. Healthcare services can have regulatory and trust barriers, but a mixed holding company only passes when the healthcare asset is material, focused, and independently defensible.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain healthcare-service companies only when clinical trust, regulatory qualification, network scale, or proprietary care assets are clearly material to the listed-company thesis.
2. Reject mixed conglomerates when healthcare exposure is not enough to offset unrelated business complexity or weak business focus.
3. Do not treat a healthcare label as sufficient without source-backed operating moat evidence.
