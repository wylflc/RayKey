# 1. A-Share Phosphate Fertilizer Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews phosphate fertilizer companies after the broader phosphorus-chemical group. Fertilizer is commodity-cycle exposed, so retention requires phosphate-resource ownership, integrated chemical chain, or material/new-energy optionality beyond fertilizer volume.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain phosphate fertilizer companies only when phosphate resources and integrated chemical/material capability are material.
2. Reject pure fertilizer capacity or weak regional fertilizer producers when resource and process differentiation is absent.
3. Compare retained fertilizer names against the broader phosphorus-chemical leaders to avoid duplicative weak peers.
