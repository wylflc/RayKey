# 1. A-Share Film And Television Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers film production and distribution, cinema chains, television drama production, digital copyright, animation/IP development, regional media assets, and ST restructuring cases.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only scarce regulated distribution channels, national infrastructure, or repeatable IP-production platforms where a funded entrant cannot easily reproduce channel status, content pipeline, or audience trust.
2. Treat cinema chains and single-project studios as low-retention unless they control an irreplaceable platform rather than simply operating screens or depending on film hits.
3. Reject ST, turnaround, regional, or hit-cycle content cases when stronger retained companies already cover the durable channel/IP thesis.
