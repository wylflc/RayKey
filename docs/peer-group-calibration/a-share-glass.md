# 1. A-Share Glass Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews float glass, PV glass, glass equipment, building/deep-processing glass, and pharmaceutical glass packaging. The industry is cyclical, but selected high-end process and customer-qualified niches can still deserve observation.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when integrated high-end glass and material platforms have breadth and process capability beyond commodity capacity.
2. Retain boundary cases for scaled PV glass, glass equipment, and pharmaceutical packaging when product qualification or process know-how is visible.
3. Reject ordinary building, appliance, daily, or generic float glass capacity when stronger retained peers cover the durable thesis.
