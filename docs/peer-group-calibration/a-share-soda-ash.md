# 1. A-Share Soda Ash Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews soda ash and related salt-chemical companies. Soda ash is commodity-cycle exposed, so retention requires a structural cost advantage such as natural alkali/trona resources, not merely current profitability or commodity recovery.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain natural-alkali/resource-backed soda ash leaders when resource quality and cost position are hard to replicate.
2. Reject synthetic soda ash or salt-chemical peers when the advantage is mainly capacity, location, or cycle timing.
3. Keep retained names as strategic-resource-cycle observations rather than valuation calls.
