# 1. A-Share Nonmetal New Materials Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group mixes high-purity quartz, electronic powders, stealth materials, battery ceramic materials, carbon-carbon composites, mica insulation, glass fiber, graphite electrodes, industrial silicon, synthetic diamonds, and weak commodity materials. The review separates customer-qualified strategic materials from commodity cycles and overcapacity.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when semiconductor, aerospace, defense, high-purity quartz, stealth, or other strategic-material qualification is central and capital cannot compress the learning curve quickly.
2. Retain boundary cases when battery, electronic ceramic, carbon-carbon, mica insulation, or advanced powder capability is real but end-market cyclicality or concentration requires caution.
3. Reject glass fiber, graphite electrodes, industrial silicon, synthetic diamond, and other commodity material producers when capacity and price cycles dominate the thesis.

## 1.4 Sources

Aggregator company introductions are not treated as decisive evidence.
