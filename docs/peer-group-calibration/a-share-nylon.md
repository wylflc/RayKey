# 1. A-Share Nylon Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers nylon engineering services, nylon filament and fabrics, caprolactam/nylon-6 integration, and functional textile applications.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary cases when process engineering, polymerization know-how, recycling routes, high-end fabric integration, or customer-qualified functional textiles create capabilities beyond commodity nylon capacity.
2. Reject generic filament, caprolactam, or mixed textile capacity when scale can be replicated with capital and the thesis is mainly cycle recovery.
