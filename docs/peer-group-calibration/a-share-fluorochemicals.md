# 1. A-Share Fluorochemicals Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews fluorite resources, refrigerants, fluorochemical chains, and lithium/electronic fluorine materials. Retention depends on scarce fluorite resources, regulatory quota, integrated chains, or high-end material/process know-how rather than commodity chemical spreads alone.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain upstream fluorite resource leaders because reserve access and mining rights are not easily replicated by capital alone.
2. Retain refrigerant leaders when quota, integrated fluorochemical chains, and customer validation create barriers beyond commodity price cycles.
3. Retain high-end fluorine-material cases only when process know-how and product qualification are visible; reject ordinary fluorochemical capacity.
