# 1. A-Share Coke Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers coke, coal chemicals, hydrogen/fuel-cell transition stories, needle coke, and regional coking platforms.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only if a company controls very low-cost coal resources, monopoly infrastructure, or a proven non-coking technology platform that cannot be bought quickly by new entrants.
2. Reject ordinary coke and coal-chemical capacity because it is cyclical, environmental-policy exposed, and hard to compound despite occasional high profits.
3. Treat hydrogen, fuel-cell, graphene, or needle-coke stories as insufficient unless there is clear leadership beyond stronger retained energy/material platforms.
