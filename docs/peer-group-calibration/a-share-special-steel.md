# 1. A-Share Special Steel Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews special steel, stainless tube/strip, high-end alloy, boiler/oilfield tube, and ordinary steel-pipe companies. Special steel can have real barriers when customer qualification, metallurgy, purity control, and aerospace/energy certification are central.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they are broad high-end special-steel platforms with product breadth, metallurgy know-how, customer qualification and scale.
2. Retain boundary cases when niche stainless, superalloy, aerospace/energy tube or high-end alloy capability is visible but platform breadth is narrower.
3. Reject commodity pipe, stainless strip, regional steel, and turnaround names when current profitability or capacity scale is the main story.

## 1.4 Sources

This first-pass peer-group review uses the local full-coverage score fields as background metadata and records official exchange disclosure landing pages in each decision row. Aggregator company introductions are not treated as decisive evidence. Later deep-company reviews should use annual reports, exchange announcements, official investor-relations materials, product certifications, customer qualification evidence, market-share evidence, segment margins, and reputable institutional research where needed.
