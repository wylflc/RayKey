# 1. A-Share Architectural-Design Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews architecture and design-consulting companies. The peer group is labor- and project-driven; retention requires more than design qualification.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only when the company has transport/infrastructure technical depth, data/tooling, or statutory/testing qualification comparable with stronger retained consulting cases.
2. Reject ordinary architectural and interior-design firms when hiring teams and bidding can replicate the capability.
3. Reject project-based design firms even when margins look acceptable in a favorable cycle.
