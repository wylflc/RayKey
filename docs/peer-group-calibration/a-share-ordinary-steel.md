# 1. A-Share Ordinary Steel Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews ordinary carbon-steel and stainless-steel producers. Steel capacity, blast furnaces, and regional customers are capital intensive but not automatically durable moats; the review retains only high-end product mix, automotive/electrical-steel certification, and globally relevant manufacturing discipline.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only steel companies whose high-end product mix, process control, customer qualification, and low-cost scale would be hard for a new entrant to match quickly.
2. Reject ordinary long products, regional steel mills, stainless or tube producers, and turnaround cases when the thesis is mainly commodity cycle recovery or local scale.
3. Do not treat crude-steel output or revenue size as moat unless it produces demonstrable high-end category control or customer switching cost.
