## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain storage-equipment companies when they own clear digital-energy, UPS/data-center power, power-electronics, storage-system, or special/aerospace power platforms with hard engineering and customer barriers.
2. Retain boundary cases only when they own a distinct BMS/PACK, Chint-backed inverter/storage, advanced battery-material, lithium-equipment, nuclear/safety power, or consumer-battery brand niche not already covered by stronger leaders.
3. Reject ordinary battery manufacturing, lead-acid replacement markets, weak storage integrators, late pivots, small primary-battery makers, and weaker power-electronics followers when stronger retained peers cover the durable thesis.
4. ST status is not an automatic rejection reason; ST逸飞 and *ST特迅 are retained as boundary cases because the company-level technology niche is distinct.
