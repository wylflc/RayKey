# 1. A-Share Other Light Industry Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This mixed light-industry group contains artificial turf, optical lenses, molded fiber, hygiene products, wigs, disposable tableware, sanitary ware, windows, oral care, glassware, and decorative materials. Most are consumer or manufacturing niches with modest barriers; retention requires global niche leadership, brand/standard power, or technical product differentiation.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary cases when global niche share, certification, product standards, or category brand creates a defensible position.
2. Reject ordinary consumer goods, hygiene products, sanitary ware, OEM/disposable goods, and housing-cycle products when competitors can copy product and channel with capital.
3. High current margins in a small niche are not enough unless the source is brand, qualification, standards, or hard process capability.
