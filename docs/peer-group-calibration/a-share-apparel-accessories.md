# 1. A-Share Apparel Accessories Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group mixes footwear OEM, garment accessories, leather, shoes, bags, and weak apparel brands. Retention requires global customer qualification, hard manufacturing systems, brand/channel durability, or a differentiated niche that stronger peers do not already cover.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain global footwear or garment-accessory leaders when customer qualification, production systems, overseas capacity, and quality control are hard to reproduce quickly.
2. Retain a smaller niche only when it has clear category leadership or customer stickiness that is not covered by a stronger retained peer.
3. Reject weak apparel brands, leather makers, bag makers, and low-level accessories when capital and execution can reproduce the business.
