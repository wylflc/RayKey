# 1. A-Share Building Construction Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers general building construction, regional contractors, metallurgical engineering, stone/lithium optionality, high-tech-zone construction transitions, and property-linked construction cases.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only when building-construction exposure is attached to a global or national platform whose qualifications, supply-chain orchestration and financing access cannot be rebuilt quickly.
2. Reject regional contractors and ordinary construction/property cases where the edge is mainly bidding, local relationships and working capital.
3. Consider cross-industry transitions, but require evidence of leadership rather than merely a stated technology or resource optionality.
