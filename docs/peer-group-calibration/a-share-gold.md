# 1. A-Share Gold Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass completes the still-undecided companies in the gold peer group. Zijin Mining, Shandong Gold, and Zhongjin Gold were already decided in the strategic-resource calibration; this file does not duplicate them. The review tests whether a company has a distinct resource or precious-metal processing thesis beyond gold-price beta.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when gold exposure is combined with strategic antimony, tungsten, silver, or other scarce metals, integrated mining-processing capability, and a role not already covered by Zijin Mining.
2. Retain boundary cases when precious-metal smelting/refining, rare-metal recovery, or silver/lead-zinc resource positions create a differentiated capability rather than simple gold-price leverage.
3. Reject ordinary pure-gold miners, regional gold assets, jewelry/ST cases, and mixed technology/mining stories when no company-level global cost, reserve, exploration, or strategic-metal edge is visible.
4. Commodity gold beta is not enough; resource quality, mine development, by-product strategic metals, recovery technology, or processing capability must be company-specific.
