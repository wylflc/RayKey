# 1. A-Share Conglomerates Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group contains mixed conglomerates and cross-industry holdings. The group label is not analytically useful, so each company is reviewed for material hard-to-replicate assets such as electronic materials, battery materials, pharmaceuticals, or strategic industrial platforms.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain conglomerates only when a material business line has hard technology, strategic-material, or customer-qualification barriers that are not already covered by stronger listed peers.
2. Reject mixed investment or local operating companies when the thesis is conglomerate optionality, asset value, or weak unrelated business spread.
3. Apply cross-industry advantage review before rejecting companies with meaningful non-conglomerate technology or strategic-material assets.
