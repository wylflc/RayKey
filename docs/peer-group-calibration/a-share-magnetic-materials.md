# 1. A-Share Magnetic Materials Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers rare-earth permanent magnets, soft magnetic powder cores, amorphous/nanocrystalline magnetic alloys, flexible magnetic materials, ferrites, magnetic components, and smaller duplicate magnet makers.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies with global high-performance rare-earth permanent-magnet leadership, broad downstream validation, or hard-to-replace roles in EV, wind, robotics, industrial motors, and energy-saving applications.
2. Retain boundary cases when differentiated soft-magnetic, amorphous, nanocrystalline, flexible magnetic, recycling, national laboratory, or customer-qualification capability is visible.
3. Reject smaller duplicate NdFeB/ferrite/component companies when leaders cover the same thesis and the company lacks differentiated application, process, customer, or resource/recycling edge.
