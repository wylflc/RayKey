# 1. A-Share Commercial Vehicles Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews buses, heavy trucks, light commercial vehicles, semi-trailers, new-energy commercial vehicles, and related platforms.

This note records the company-by-company review for `交运设备-汽车-商用车`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain commercial-vehicle leaders with durable scale, export/channel, electrification, powertrain, or segment leadership that cannot be quickly replicated by capital alone.
2. Boundary-retain differentiated commercial-vehicle, bus, trailer, or broad platform companies when they have segment strength but are more cyclical or less dominant than direct leaders.
3. Reject weaker bus/truck makers, transition stories, and local light-commercial platforms when stronger retained peers cover the same thesis.
