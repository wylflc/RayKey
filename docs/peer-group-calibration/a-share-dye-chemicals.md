# 1. A-Share Dye And Printing Chemicals Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers dye intermediates, disperse/reactive dyes, organic pigments, textile auxiliaries, digital textile inks, high-end coatings, and printing/packaging materials.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when oligopoly position, vertical intermediates, environmental/regulatory barriers, process know-how, and global customer breadth create a durable moat.
2. Retain boundary cases for high-performance pigments, high-end coatings, or meaningful oligopoly participants when customer qualification and formulation/process know-how are visible.
3. Reject smaller duplicate dye, textile-auxiliary, pigment, ink, and troubled chemical cases when stronger retained peers already cover the durable thesis.
