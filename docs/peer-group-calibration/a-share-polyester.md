# 1. A-Share Polyester Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group compares polyester filament, industrial polyester, integrated refining-PTA-polyester platforms, recycled and differentiated polyester, and smaller specialty yarn cases.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain integrated platforms only when scale, refining or PTA integration, cost position, and cross-chain economics create a capability stronger than commodity polyester capacity.
2. Retain boundary industrial-textile or differentiated-polyester cases when product qualification and application know-how are more defensible than ordinary yarn capacity.
3. Reject smaller polyester filament, colored yarn, recycled-polyester, or investment-holding cases when they are weaker duplicates of retained platforms and depend mainly on commodity cycle spreads.
