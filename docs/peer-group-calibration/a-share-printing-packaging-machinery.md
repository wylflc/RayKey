## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews beverage packaging lines, automated packaging equipment, filling equipment, label/printing machinery, and related consumables. Retention requires complete-line capability, customer validation, automation/software integration, or category leadership beyond ordinary machine assembly.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain niche packaging-machinery leaders when complete-line integration, food/beverage customer validation, and automation know-how are visible.
2. Reject ordinary packaging or printing machinery when the advantage is mainly machine assembly, price, or domestic replacement without durable installed-base evidence.
3. Keep retained names as boundary cases unless the company shows global platform status.
