# 1. A-Share Tourism-Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews travel agencies, regional tourism-service operators, scenic-service companies, and cultural-tourism operators. Tourism demand can rebound cyclically, but cyclicality is not the same as durable moat.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only companies with iconic scarce resources, scalable hard-to-copy IP, or platform economics that capital cannot quickly replicate.
2. Reject travel agencies, regional tourism services, hotel and transport operators, and project tourism companies when execution and local resources are the main edge.
3. Align with the accepted scenic-spot calibration: only the strongest scarce-resource platforms deserve boundary observation.
