# 1. A-Share Aluminum Remaining Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group completes remaining aluminum names after the earlier strategic-resource decision. Aluminum is mostly commodity-cycle exposed, so retention is limited to hard resource/cost advantages, high-value processing with customer qualification, green-energy differentiation, or electronic/material niches.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain aluminum companies only when resource/cost integration, high-value processing, green-energy aluminum, or electronic-material capability is materially differentiated.
2. Reject commodity smelting, generic extrusion, foil, recycling, or project-recovery stories when a better retained peer covers the thesis.
3. Do not treat scale alone as moat in aluminum; the company must pass the capital-replicability and dominance tests.
