# 1. A-Share Petroleum And Gas Trade Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews oil-products, LNG, gas-trading, and regional fuel-distribution names. The default is low-barrier rejection unless a listed company owns scarce upstream resources, irreplaceable terminals/pipelines, or a regulated network with long-lived economics.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Reject pure petroleum, gas, LNG, or fuel trading when margins depend mainly on sourcing spreads, logistics, or local retail execution.
2. Retain only companies with scarce upstream resources, irreplaceable terminals, pipelines, or regulated city-gas style networks.
3. Do not allow a high current margin or local license to override the capital-replicability test when stronger infrastructure evidence is absent.
