# 1. A-Share Trade Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews commodity traders, supply-chain distributors, electronic-component distributors, aviation-material distributors, and market operators. The default is strict because trade businesses often earn working-capital and execution returns.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain unique market-infrastructure platforms when location, merchant network, platform data, and operating rights create hard-to-copy control points.
2. Reject ordinary commodity, electronics, IT, and consumer-goods distributors when customer switching, supplier substitution, and low margins dominate.
3. Reject parent-company or duplicate exposure when a stronger listed subsidiary or peer already covers the real technology/material thesis.
