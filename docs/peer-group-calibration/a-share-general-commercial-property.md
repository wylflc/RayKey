# 1. A-Share General Commercial-Property Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews general commercial-property operators. Property assets may have value, but this screening is for durable company capability rather than asset appraisal or real-estate recovery.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only if a company owns scarce infrastructure-like assets or a platform whose economics cannot be replicated by capital.
2. Reject local malls, commercial leases, ST property cases, and mixed property operations when the moat is mainly location or balance sheet.
3. Do not treat low valuation or property-recovery optionality as business-quality evidence.
