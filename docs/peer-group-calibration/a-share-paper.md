# 1. A-Share Paper And Paper-Based Materials Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews pulp and paper, specialty paper, decorative base paper, aramid paper, tissue, packaging board, and paper-based functional materials.

This note records the company-by-company review for `轻工制造-造纸印刷-造纸`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain integrated pulp-paper or specialty paper-material leaders with scale, cost curve, product breadth, process technology, and customer qualification advantages.
2. Boundary-retain focused specialty-paper or functional-material companies when product technology, customer qualification, or category leadership is distinct from commodity paper cycles.
3. Reject commodity packaging/cultural paper, tissue brands, regional mills, ST cases, and small paper-product companies when the thesis is mostly capacity, pulp cycle, or local cost position.
