# 1. A-Share Supermarkets Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews supermarket and grocery retailers. The category is competitive, low-margin, and vulnerable to new formats and online/local-service platforms.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only if a supermarket platform has overwhelming category control or a proprietary supply-chain model that capital cannot quickly copy.
2. Reject local and national supermarket operators when store network and procurement scale are the main advantages.
3. Do not treat rebound from losses or store closure cycles as moat evidence.
