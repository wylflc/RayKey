# 1. A-Share Real Estate Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group contains property management, real-estate brokerage, local commercial assets, service outsourcing, and distressed real-estate-service companies. The industry is labor-intensive, parent/project dependent, and exposed to real-estate structural headwinds, so the group can reasonably have no watchlist companies.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Reject ordinary property management, brokerage, commercial-property service, parking, cleaning, and local real-estate service companies when they lack irreplaceable technology, data, or national platform control.
2. Parent-company relationships, local government projects, and service contracts are not enough if margins are labor-intensive and easily competed away.
3. Allow whole-group rejection when the industry mainly earns execution fees and does not show durable pricing power.
