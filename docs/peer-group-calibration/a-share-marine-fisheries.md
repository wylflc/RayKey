# 1. A-Share Marine Fisheries Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers offshore fishing, marine aquaculture, seafood processing, distant-water fishing, and distressed or mixed seafood platforms.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only if a company controls scarce fishing rights, biological breeding capability, global protein-brand/channel assets, or a hard-to-replicate cold-chain platform with durable economics.
2. Reject commodity aquaculture, wild-catch, seafood processing, and ST/mixed cases when earnings depend mainly on resource cycle, biological risk, commodity price, or governance rather than a durable company moat.
