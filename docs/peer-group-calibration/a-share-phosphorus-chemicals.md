# 1. A-Share Phosphorus Chemicals Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews phosphate rock, wet-process phosphoric acid, fine phosphorus chemicals, fertilizer, and related new-energy material exposure. Retention requires resource reserves plus process integration or high-value chemical/material capability.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain integrated phosphorus-resource leaders when phosphate rock, fine chemicals, and process capability reinforce each other.
2. Retain boundary specialists when wet-process purification, feed/industrial phosphates, or new-energy material routes are differentiated but narrower.
3. Reject ordinary phosphate fertilizer or commodity phosphorus chemical producers when stronger retained peers cover the resource and process thesis.
