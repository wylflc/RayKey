# 1. A-Share Online Shopping Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group contains e-commerce brand-operation services, consumer-decision content/data platforms, and cross-border e-commerce/SaaS/logistics businesses. The review separates user data and consumer-decision workflow from platform-dependent operations.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary cases when consumer content, data, and decision workflow create defensible traffic and user trust that is not simply bought through advertising spend.
2. Reject e-commerce agency, brand operations, and cross-border selling models when dependence on third-party platforms, brands, traffic costs, and logistics execution weakens the moat.
3. Online commerce needs owned user attention, data, workflow, or network effects; merchant operations or traffic buying alone is not enough.
4. AI or SaaS language is not sufficient unless it is tied to a hard-to-replicate dataset or customer workflow.
