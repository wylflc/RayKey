# 1. A-Share Small Appliances Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group includes cleaning appliances, kitchen appliances, cookware, kitchen electricals, small-appliance ODMs, personal-care appliances, massage devices, and smart home/kitchen products. The category often has low product barriers and short cycles, so retention requires a durable brand, channel, product engineering, or motor/core-component capability.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary companies when category brand, channel/service network, product engineering, motor/core-component capability, or parent/global customer ecosystem creates durable advantage.
2. Reject fashion-driven small appliances, commodity ODM, weak kitchen-appliance followers, and low-tech personal-care appliances when a well-funded entrant can copy product and channel.
3. In appliance categories, brand and service network can be a moat, but only for category leaders or companies with distinctive engineering capability.
