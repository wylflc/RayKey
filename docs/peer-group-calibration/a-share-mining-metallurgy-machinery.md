# 1. A-Share Mining And Metallurgy Machinery Peer-Group Review
## 1.1 Purpose
This note records the company-by-company review for `机械设备-专用设备-矿山冶金机械`.

This group contains coal-mining machinery, mine trucks, oilfield equipment, mineral-processing equipment, metallurgy automation, heavy forgings, crushing equipment, and electrorefining equipment. The review separates scarce mining automation, safety-critical equipment, national heavy-equipment platforms, and process-equipment niches from ordinary cyclical machinery capacity.
## 1.2 Retention Standard
1. Directly retain companies with category-leading mining automation, industrial robotics, safety-critical underground equipment, or hard process automation that a funded entrant cannot quickly reproduce.
2. Boundary-retain companies with central-institute technology, national heavy-equipment capability, mining-truck or mineral-processing niches, oilfield measurement, or electrorefining process equipment, but cap conviction for cyclicality and project dependence.
3. Reject ordinary coal machinery followers, generic crushing/screening equipment, low-differentiation gear or transmission makers, ST cases, and project automation companies without scarce process control.
