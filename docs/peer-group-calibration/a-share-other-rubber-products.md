# 1. A-Share Other Rubber Products Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group mixes industrial hoses, rubber additives, automotive seals, polymer membranes, seismic isolation, conveyor belts, consumer products, and small rubber-product manufacturers. Most rubber manufacturing is replicable, so retention requires safety-critical qualification, specialty chemistry, fluid-control, membrane, or building-safety barriers.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain boundary companies when high-pressure fluid connectors, rubber additives, ePTFE/membrane materials, or seismic isolation products have qualification, safety, customer workflow, or specialty process barriers.
2. Reject ordinary rubber seals, tubes, belts, consumer plastic/rubber products, OEM capacity, and small processors when capital and customer qualification can be replicated by stronger peers.
3. Commodity rubber or chemical-cycle exposure is not enough without a niche where product failure cost, formulation, or certification creates switching cost.
