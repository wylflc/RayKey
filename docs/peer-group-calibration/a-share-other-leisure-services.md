# 1. A-Share Other Leisure-Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews exhibition, cultural-production, and other leisure-service companies. These businesses can show good project execution, but project execution is not automatically a durable moat.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only companies with scarce assets, hard IP, platform network effects, or customer workflow control that capital cannot quickly replicate.
2. Reject event, exhibition, and cultural-production service businesses when revenue is project-based and team execution is the main advantage.
3. Allow whole-group rejection when industry structure is low-barrier.
