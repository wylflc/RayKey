# 1. A-Share Plastic Products Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This broad group covers special engineering plastics, functional films, PI films, modified plastics, synthetic leather, PMMA/optical materials, PV and electronic functional films, biolab plastic consumables, magnetic-plastic appliance components, protective polymer components, packaging, household plastics, recycled plastics, molds, and ST or transition cases.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they have hard-to-copy high-performance polymer, special engineering plastic, global functional-film, or PI-film capability with process know-how and customer qualification.
2. Retain boundary cases when a niche has visible leadership, certification, customer embedding, or product performance that makes capital-only entry difficult: smart-card substrates, high-performance BOPP/PET films, PV/optical adhesive materials, modified and ICT materials, PU synthetic leather leaders, PMMA/MS optical materials, TPE/TPU, lab consumables, appliance magnetic seals, or engineering-plastic components.
3. Reject ordinary packaging, household plastic products, injection molds, generic modified plastics, recycled plastics, cable materials, foam, weak PV-film followers, ST cases, and transition stories where stronger retained peers cover the material thesis.
