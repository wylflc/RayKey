# 1. A-Share Packaging And Printing Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group includes premium consumer-electronics packaging, flexible packaging, metal cans, cigarette packaging, security printing, aseptic packaging, paper cartons, PET bottles, plastic caps, and small printing cases. Most packaging is low margin and easy to replicate, so retention requires customer qualification, design/process integration, material know-how, or category substitution power.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain premium packaging platforms when design, material engineering, automated manufacturing, global customer qualification, and embedded supply-chain workflow create switching costs.
2. Retain boundary cases when flexible or aseptic packaging has real material/process know-how and customer qualification.
3. Reject ordinary paper cartons, metal cans, PET bottles, tobacco labels, caps, small printing, and low-margin packaging capacity when stronger retained peers already cover the durable thesis.
