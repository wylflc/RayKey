# 1. A-Share Chlor-Alkali Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews PVC, caustic soda, chlor-alkali, and integrated energy-chemical companies. Chlor-alkali is mostly commodity capacity; retention requires integrated low-cost energy/resource position, circular economy, or cross-business capability that is not easily replicated.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain chlor-alkali companies only when low-cost energy/resource integration and circular-economy platform advantages are clear.
2. Reject ordinary PVC, caustic soda, or regional chlor-alkali producers when the advantage is capacity or cycle timing.
3. Keep retained names as boundary observations because commodity spread exposure remains high.
