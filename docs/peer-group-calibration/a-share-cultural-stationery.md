# 1. A-Share Cultural Stationery Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews stationery, office supplies, and creative cultural products. Most products are easy to copy, so retention requires brand pull, dense channels, category leadership, or a differentiated distribution system rather than manufacturing capacity alone.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only the category brand and channel leader when the industry is mature and product technology is modest.
2. Reject office-supply platforms, exporters, and paper-stationery producers when scale can be challenged by e-commerce platforms or stronger brands.
3. Treat brand/channel power as a moat only when it is clearly stronger than peers and not just current revenue size.
