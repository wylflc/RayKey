## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers landscape construction, ecological restoration, municipal landscaping, environmental landscape projects, and ST project contractors.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only if proprietary restoration technology, regulated ecological assets, data/operation platforms, or recurring infrastructure concessions create durable barriers.
2. Reject ordinary landscape construction and ecological project contractors when bidding, local relationships, design teams, and working capital are easy for funded competitors to replicate.
3. ST status and receivable/project-cycle risk raise the evidence bar further.
