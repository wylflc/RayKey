# 1. A-Share Civil Explosives Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group covers civil-explosive manufacturers, electronic detonators, blasting services, mine services, civil-explosive equipment/informatization, regional license holders, and military-adjacent transitions.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they are national civil-explosive or mine-service platforms with integrated production, blasting, mine construction, overseas execution, or defense optionality.
2. Retain boundary cases when intelligent equipment, information systems, electronic detonators, or regional full-chain capability create a differentiated regulated niche.
3. Reject small regional license holders, ST cases, and weaker duplicates when the license is local and stronger retained platforms cover the sector thesis.
