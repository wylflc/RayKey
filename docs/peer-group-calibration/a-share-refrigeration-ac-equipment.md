# 1. A-Share Refrigeration And Air-Conditioning Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This pass reviews refrigeration compressors, industrial cooling, air-cooling exchangers, HVAC components, data-center cooling, cold-chain equipment, and commercial refrigeration.

This note records the company-by-company review for `机械设备-通用设备-制冷空调设备`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Directly retain process-critical refrigeration/cooling platforms with compressor, nuclear/power/petrochemical cooling, HVAC valve, or high-end thermal-management capability.
2. Boundary-retain differentiated industrial refrigeration, hydrogen/cooling, data-center liquid cooling, high-reliability ventilation, scroll-compressor, or energy-saving equipment niches.
3. Reject ordinary commercial refrigeration, catering equipment, appliance HVAC parts, filters, cold-chain cabinets, and component suppliers when capacity and customer relationships are replicable.
