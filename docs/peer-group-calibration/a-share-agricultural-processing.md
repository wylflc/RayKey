# 1. A-Share Agricultural Processing Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group contains plant extraction, edible fungi, konjac processing, food additives, sugar, edible oil, grain/oil processing, fruit/vegetable distribution, and distressed agricultural processors. Most agricultural processing has weak pricing power; retention requires global niche share, biological/extraction process capability, or brand/category control.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain plant-extract and specialty-food-ingredient companies when raw-material sourcing, extraction process, customer qualification, and global niche share are visible.
2. Retain boundary niche leaders only when category control and process capability are stronger than ordinary crop processing.
3. Reject edible oil, sugar, grain, mushroom, fruit/vegetable, and commodity food-processing businesses when they are mainly scale or commodity spread businesses.
