# 1. A-Share Remaining Other Chemical Products Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for the remaining companies in `基础化工-化学制品-其他化学制品`.

This is a broad residual chemical group. It mixes organic silicone, titanium dioxide and titanium/zirconium chemicals, electronic gases, separation membranes and resins, OLED and liquid-crystal materials, food ingredients, synthetic biology, chromatography media, adhesives, civil-engineering materials, polymer additives, cellulose ether, silanes, fluorochemicals, wet electronic chemicals, photoresist-related materials, catalysts, cosmetic active ingredients, commodity petrochemicals, carbon black, fertilizers, coatings, fragrances, and ST transition cases.

The review separates scarce material platforms from ordinary chemical capacity. Current earnings are secondary; the central test is whether a well-funded entrant could quickly reproduce the company's formulation, process, customer qualification, resource integration, or application know-how.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they have platform-level material capability: high-end organic silicone, integrated titanium/zirconium chemistry, electronic bulk gases, adsorption and ion-exchange resins, membrane separation, OLED/liquid-crystal materials, high-share food ingredients, synthetic-biology fermentation, chromatography media, high-end fluorochemicals, or molecular-sieve materials.
2. Retain boundary cases when a company has a differentiated niche with visible customer validation or process know-how: reflective and optical films, PVA integration, defense-adjacent nitrocellulose, high-end adhesives, electronic chemicals, concrete admixtures, polymer stabilizers, silica materials, industrial biocides, cellulose ether, organosilicon additives, silanes, fluorochemicals, photoresist-related chemicals, wet electronic chemicals, catalyst systems, pharmaceutical excipients, process-package technology, wet-metallurgy extractants, cosmetic actives, silicon chemical chains, or battery electrolyte additives.
3. Reject commodity titanium dioxide followers, carbon black, petrochemicals, fertilizers, coatings and pigments, generic adhesives and tapes, low-barrier water-treatment chemicals, common PVC/polymer additives, fragrance suppliers, smaller catalyst duplicates, chemical reagents, weak battery-chemical followers, and ST or transition cases.
4. Do not reject scarce materials because a cyclical trough depresses current profit. Do reject businesses where normalized economics are driven mainly by commodity price, capacity utilization, or construction/manufacturing cycle rather than a hard-to-copy capability.
