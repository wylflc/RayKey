# 1. A-Share Lifting And Transport Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This group reviews port machinery, logistics lifting equipment, parking automation, conveyors, cranes, and rail/industrial components. Most capacity is project or manufacturing-cycle exposed, so retention requires global installed base, safety-critical qualification, or hard-to-copy system integration.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain global port or heavy-equipment platforms when installed base, system integration, customer qualification, and international project references are visible.
2. Reject ordinary cranes, conveyors, parking systems, and handling equipment when capacity, engineering execution, or local orders are the main advantage.
3. Treat current profit recovery as insufficient unless the company owns a durable niche that stronger peers cannot cover.
