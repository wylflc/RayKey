# Final Position And Rebalance Plan

Updated: 2026-06-15

This file is the consolidated final position plan after the core watchlist valuation review. It replaces earlier draft files such as `current_position_selection_plan.md` and `core_watchlist_position_plan.csv`.

## 1. Assumptions

- Current known A-share/HK-share stock exposure is about `174.3%` of net assets, based on the user's stated positions.
- This plan does not reduce or increase total gross exposure. It only reallocates relative stock weights.
- Target weights are shown in two ways:
  - `Stock Exposure Weight`: percentage of total stock exposure, summing to 100%.
  - `Net Asset Weight`: approximate percentage of net assets if total exposure stays near `174.3%`.
- Business quality and current valuation are separated. High-quality companies can remain in the watchlist even when not included in the final portfolio.

- Reduce BYD out of the final six-stock portfolio and use the proceeds to complete the target weights.

Practical transition:

- If unwilling to exit BYD immediately, first reduce it to a temporary `15%-20%` net-asset watch position.
- Reassess after Q3/Q4 evidence on overseas profit, blade-battery supply, intelligent-driving adoption, and margin recovery.
- If the thesis is not clearly validated, finish the switch into the six-stock target portfolio.

| Security | Add / Hold Evidence | Reduce / Exit Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tencent | Games, ads, video accounts, FCF, and buybacks remain strong; valuation remains below platform quality. | Regulation tightens, ads/games stagnate, AI spending compresses profit, or buybacks weaken while valuation rises. |
